# Green Travel WebApp

Introduction:
Green Traveler’s is a web-app through which users can search for their desired destination and tourism activities based on tourist places. 
Problem Definition:
Most of the foreigners faces multiple problems like they cannot find specific place to stay, any traditional foods, hotel bookings, tourist places etc. People who live in Dhaka, also those who doesn’t travel much faces many problems like where to go or how to go to that specific location. 
Motivation:
As most of the foreigners suffers because of the little knowledge of Bangladesh tourist spots. Though this app, we will provide solutions to those who are facing these problems by adding tourist spots along with hotels, restaurants, traditional foods etc.
